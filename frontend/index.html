📄 PDF Question Answering Chatbot

Upload a PDF, then ask questions about its content

👋 Hello! I'm your PDF assistant.

📌 **How to use:**
1. Click "Choose PDF File" to select a PDF
2. Click "Upload PDF" to process it
3. Type your question and click "Ask"

💡 **Example questions:**
• What is this PDF about?
• What is the PDF name?
• Summarize the main points
